A partial ordering tells us which of two things 'come first'

 

RAT

Reflexive, Antisymmetric, Transitive

 

 

 

 

 

A set with a partial order is a partially ordered set, or a poset, denoted by (A, [po])

 

A partial order in which every two elements are comparable is a total order, or a linear order
